# CRM Revenue Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

##  Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **Revenue Analysis Dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze company performance across regions, industries, and company segments.

The dashboard transforms raw CRM data into meaningful insights to support data-driven business decisions.

---

##  Objective
To analyze revenue performance by:
- Region
- Industry (Sector)
- Company Establishment Period

And identify key business insights and growth opportunities.

---

##  Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Data Cleaning & Transformation
- Data Visualization

---

##  Key Features
- KPI cards for Total Revenue, Average Revenue, Highest Revenue, and Total Companies
- Revenue analysis by Region
- Industry-wise revenue distribution
- Revenue distribution by company establishment period
- Top 10 companies by revenue
- Interactive slicers for filtering (Region, Year, Industry)

---

##  Data Processing
- Cleaned and transformed raw CRM dataset
- Handled missing and inconsistent values
- Created calculated measures using DAX
- Structured data for efficient visualization

---

##  Key Insights
- The United States is the dominant revenue contributor, indicating strong regional concentration.
- Software and Technology sectors generate the highest revenue.
- Revenue is highly dependent on a small group of top-performing companies.
- Older companies contribute significantly more revenue than newer companies.
- Revenue distribution is uneven, with a large gap between top and average performers.

---

##  Business Recommendations
- Expand operations in underperforming regions to reduce geographic dependency.
- Diversify into low-performing industries to unlock new growth opportunities.
- Strengthen engagement with mid-tier and smaller companies.
- Develop targeted strategies for newly established companies.
- Implement strong client retention strategies for high-value accounts.
